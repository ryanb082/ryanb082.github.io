The head elf in charge of Santa’s workshop (Noel) has locked up the factory and sealed all of Christmas town with perplexing puzzles. He’s convinced that since nobody enjoys the spirit of the holidays any more they might as well be cancelled.

Santa has enlisted you, people of pure and noble heart, to teleport to the North Pole and solve the puzzles. Can you melt Noel’s icy heart by making his secret holiday wish come true? Work together, revive the town, and save Christmas before time runs out!
